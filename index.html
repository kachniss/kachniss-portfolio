About me

I am a Front End Web Developer in Vancouver, BC. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and WordPress development.

Ever since I was in elementary school, I have been passionate about leaning, trying and creating things, and even back then I saw myself studying and working in the tech field. I decided to study Biomedical Engineering after graduating high school. It gave me the opportunity to learn and to work on projects from a very broad spectrum. It made me realize that with the right mindset, every skill can be learned and every project or task can be finished, no matter how hard or challenging it seems. It also introduced me to the world of web application programming, which I fell in love with.

A lot has changed since I started taking first steps towards becoming a web developer. I gained experience working in various different fields and positions that taught me a lot about myself, about working hard and working for the team. I moved from Czech Republic to a different continent to pursue my dream. I learned a lot of new - not only programming - languages and technical skills. But one thing is the same - my attitude. I know that I still have a lot to learn but I am not afraid of any challenges I will face as a web developer because I know it is exactly what I want to do.

I'm still building and improving this portfolio. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me.
